Is generative 'AI' threatening your job? If there's any truth in the hype, you'd almost think so. But it's not that simple, tech worker Roza Kuiper explains in a new article for *Jacobin*.

There's a huge gap between AI promises and what the technology actually delivers. It's unlikely that it'll take your job anytime soon.

Yet, that doesn't mean everything's fine. As Kuiper writes:

> The fact that a technology now exists that claims to be able to replace workers (even though it can't) gives companies an excuse to put pressure on labourers' working conditions.

Regardless of whether it works or not, bosses and managers will try to use AI as an excuse to convert full-time jobs into precarious *gig* work. That means that as a worker, you must stay alert.
