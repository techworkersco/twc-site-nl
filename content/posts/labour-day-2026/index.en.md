On the 1st of May, workers all over the world celebrate Labour Day (c'mon Netherlands, where's that national holiday‽). Techwerkers is joining in too, and you're invited! Come to the Techwerkers table at Museumplein in Amsterdam, starting at 1:00pm.

Here's what to expect:
- **Test your mesh!** Bring your [meshcore device](/en/resources/intro-mesh-network-communication/) and test it out in the wild with other workers. Setup support will also be on hand
- **AI bingo:** Take a break from the so-called 'AI' hype. Turn your boss and managers' clichés to good use with a game of AI implementation bingo.
- **Zines & stickers** on safe(r) communication, tips for organizing at work, and much more.

And of course a friendly, low-key occasion to chat and meet other people in tech in the Netherlands.

Are you joining? 😀

[Add the event to your calendar.](https://events.techwerkers.nl/event/labour-day-or-dag-van-de-arbeid)
